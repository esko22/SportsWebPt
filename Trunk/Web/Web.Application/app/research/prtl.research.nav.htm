Injuries

Injury Pages are designed to educate individuals on basic information regarding common orthopedic injuries. Each page contains general information, common causes and signs, prognoses, and rehabilitation plans for that specific injury.

Plans

Our Rehabiliation, Prevention, and Stretching/Strengthening Plans were intelligently designed by SWPT Physical Therapists to allow an individual with a busy schedule to be expedite recovery from injury in a realistic time frame. Please use the exercises with sets/repetitions/and times as a guideline for your recovery.

Exercises

SportsWebPT’s videos are designed to educate individuals on proper technique to maximize the effectiveness of each individual exercise. Each video contains the base form on how to properly perform the exercise. Videos also contains common mistakes which should be avoided by the user, as well as common progressions to challenge the user if the exercise becomes too easy.
