Privacy

The privacy and security of the Personal Information you provide us is our highest priority. We use industry standard security and encryption with hosting redundancy in the US to provide continuous access to our service and to protect your Personal Information.

When creating a profile, we require your first name, last name, city, state, zip code, email address and a unique password. In order to tailor our product to your needs, we may request the following information: age, sex, occupation, hobbies and social media accounts. None of your personal information will be displayed publically on the Site.

We will not share your Personal Information with any third parties. We will only use your Personal Information to send notifications that you have requested.

SportsWebPT will communicate to you via email or messages posted on your User Dashboard. You may opt out of receiving notifications at any time by updating your profile information.
