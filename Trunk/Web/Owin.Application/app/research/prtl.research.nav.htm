Injuries

Injury pages are designed to educate individuals on basic information regarding common orthopedic injuries. Each page contains general information, common causes and signs, prognoses, and rehabilitation plans for your specific injury.

Plans

Our Rehabilitation, Prevention, and Stretching/Strengthening Plans were intelligently designed by SWPT Physical Therapists to allow individuals with a busy schedule to expedite recovery in a realistic time frame. Please use the exercises with sets, repetitions and times as a guideline for your recovery.

Exercises

SportsWebPT videos are designed to educate individuals on proper technique to maximize the effectiveness of each exercise. Each video details the correct technique for performing the exercise. Videos also contain common mistakes that should be avoided by the user, as well as progressions to challenge the user if the exercise becomes too easy.
